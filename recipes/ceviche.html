Ceviche

Description:

Fresh, zesty, and full of life! Ceviche is Peru’s pride—raw fish “cooked” in citrus, mixed with onion, chili, and a pop of corn and sweet potato. A refreshing explosion of flavor!

Ingredients:

  • Beef sirloin (sliced)
  • Fresh white fish (cubed)
  • Lime juice
  • Red onion (sliced)
  • Chili pepper (aji limo or similar)
  • Salt
  • Cilantro
  • Corn (cooked)
  • weet potato (boiled)

Steps:

  1. Marinate fish in lime juice for 10–15 minutes.
  2. Add onion, chili, salt, and cilantro.
  3. Let it all mingle for a few more minutes.
  4. Serve with corn and sweet potato.
  5. Enjoy the sunshine in every bite!

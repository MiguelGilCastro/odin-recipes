Seco de carne

Description:

A hearty, happy stew that warms the heart! Seco de carne is a slow-cooked beef dish with cilantro sauce, potatoes, and carrots—comfort food with a Peruvian twist!

Ingredients:

  • Beef chunks
  • Chopped onion
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro (blended with a bit of water)
  • Yellow chili or aji amarillo paste
  • Beer or broth
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooked white rice

Steps:

  1. Brown the beef and set aside
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, and chili paste
  3. Add blended cilantro, then return the beef
  4. Pour in beer or broth, add veggies
  5. Simmer until tender
  6. Serve with rice—and good vibes!

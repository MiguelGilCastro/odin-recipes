Lomo saltado

Description:

A joyful stir-fry that brings together the best of Peruvian and Chinese flavors! Juicy beef, colorful veggies, and crispy fries dance in a savory soy sauce. Quick, tasty, and full of soul!

Ingredients:

  • Beef sirloin (sliced)
  • Red onion (sliced)
  • Tomato (sliced)
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Yellow chili (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro
  • French fries
  • Cooked white rice

Steps:

  1. Sear the beef in a hot pan.
  2. Add onions, tomatoes, garlic, and chili.
  3. Splash in soy sauce and vinegar.
  4. Mix in fries and cilantro.
  5. Serve with rice and smile!
